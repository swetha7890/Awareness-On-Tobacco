About us



Welcome to Tobacco Awareness, where we are dedicated to spreading awareness about the dangers of tobacco use. Our mission is to empower individuals with knowledge and resources to make informed decisions for a healthier life.


Mission Statement: To create a tobacco-free future by educating, inspiring, and supporting communities worldwide.


At Tobacco Awareness, we believe in the power of education to drive positive change. Through research-based information, compelling campaigns, and community engagement, we strive to reduce the prevalence of tobacco use and its associated health risks.

Join us in our journey towards a tobacco-free world. Together, we can make a difference.

A public health system is the often-invisible web of protections that help us lead long, productive and healthy lives. It is regulations that keep toxins out of our food and pollutants out of the air we breathe. It is data that informs decisions about health policy, and systems that empower people to make healthier choices.

We’re helping governments acquire the knowledge and tools they need to make rapid progress against cancer, heart disease, obesity, drug overdose, road crashes and other leading causes of disease, injury and death. We’re working for a world where a little girl growing up in New Delhi, Cape Town or São Paolo has the same opportunity for health as a girl growing up anywhere. With your partnership, we know we can make that happen, and billions of people can live longer, healthier lives.
